About

Layal Sleiman is an architect based in Munich, Germany, holding a Master of Arts and a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture from the Lebanese University. She is a registered member of the Bayerische Architektenkammer (Chamber of Architects, Bavaria) and the Order of Engineers and Architects in Beirut.

With a proven track record in developing innovative design solutions, she brings a strong background in both research and the practical application of architecture. Her experience spans comprehensive project management — from initial site analysis and concept development through construction documentation and on-site supervision — ensuring every project meets client expectations and regulatory requirements. Her work ranges from large-scale hospitality and mixed-use developments to residential villas, interior renovations, landscape design, and brand identity.

Sanctuary in the Pines

Located within the largest pine forest in the Middle East, this medical spa is conceived as a healing retreat where nature becomes an essential part of the therapeutic experience. The project aims to support both physical and psychological well-being by creating a calm, private, and restorative environment away from the noise of everyday life.

The proposal responds to Lebanon's growing need for spaces that promote healing and mental recovery, while taking advantage of a site that is both naturally secluded and relatively accessible from the capital. Rather than opposing the land's steep topography and natural terraces, the design integrates with them, carefully adapting its forms and distributing its volumes to preserve the existing landscape and minimize tree removal.

Rooted in sustainability, the project reflects an architectural approach that works in harmony with nature, allowing the environment itself to become a central element in the process of healing.

Between Heritage and Skyline

Beirut Waterfront Mixed-Use Development is located on Beirut's seafront, facing the historic Al-Majidyeh Mosque and adjacent to Zaha Hadid's Beirut Department Stores. The project is composed of two towers with distinct functions, connected through a commercial podium that becomes the public heart of the development. The podium design reinterprets the morphology of Beirut's ancient urban fabric, creating layered public spaces, shared courtyards, and green terraces. In contrast, the towers adopt a contemporary glass expression that reflects the modern skyline, while integrating a sustainable model through planted setbacks and elevated outdoor communal spaces.

Classical Forms in the Mountains

This four-story residential villa in Jbaa rises against the backdrop of the Lebanese mountains, rooted in the traditions of classical Lebanese stone architecture. The design is defined by hand-cut limestone facades, pointed arches with tracery detailing, Corinthian columns and capitals, ornamental stone balustrades, and a red clay tile hip roof — elements that together evoke the grandeur of heritage Lebanese manor houses while serving a contemporary residential program. A sharp natural slope in the terrain presented both a challenge and an opportunity. By working with the topography rather than against it, the design leverages the incline to introduce a fully functional underground floor with one side fully exposed to the exterior, gaining natural light, ventilation, and direct garden access. This basement level, seamlessly integrated into the hillside, houses a garage and service areas while contributing to the villa's commanding presence on the landscape. The project was carried through from concept to construction, encompassing structural design, interior spatial planning across all four levels, and landscape design for the surrounding garden and terraces.

Two-Story Contemporary Villa

This two-story villa is strategically positioned on a shared plot to preserve unobstructed views while organizing the land into two distinct outdoor zones: a private garden, accessible only from the residence, and a communal garden shared with the neighboring house. The site layout maintains a clear and welcoming entrance passage without compromising either outdoor space. The architecture adopts a contemporary vocabulary of clean geometric volumes, combining white rendered walls with warm wood cladding, structured framing elements, and vertical accent fins that enhance depth and articulation. A planted rooftop pergola crowns the composition, extending greenery upward and creating an elevated outdoor retreat. Inside, the ground floor is organized around an open-plan living area connected to the kitchen and service spaces, forming a fluid and functional social zone. The upper floor accommodates three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a balcony overlooking the garden, providing private spaces with direct visual connection to the landscape.

Framing a New Identity

This renovation project reinterprets the exterior identity of an existing villa, transforming its raw unfinished concrete shell into a refined contemporary residence. The new facade introduces a clear architectural language defined by crisp white volumes, dark accent panels, warm wood detailing, and carefully framed openings that enhance depth, balance, and visual rhythm. Beyond a cosmetic upgrade, the intervention reshapes the villa's character through a more cohesive and elegant composition. The contrast of materials gives the elevation a modern yet welcoming expression, while the landscaped garden setting softens the architecture and strengthens its connection to the surrounding environment. The result is a renewed residential image that is clean, timeless, and distinctly contemporary.

Comprehensive Construction Documentation

This project presents a complete construction documentation package developed for a multi-story residential villa composed of a basement, ground floor, and upper levels. The architectural character features a traditional stone-and-brick facade, arched window openings, and wrought iron detailing that reflect a classic residential expression.

The documentation set covers all major building disciplines, beginning with site fixation and detailed architectural drawings including floor plans, elevations, and sections. It further integrates structural documentation such as foundation layouts and structural ceiling systems, alongside mechanical water supply and drainage networks, and comprehensive electrical lighting and power distribution plans.

Each drawing is developed at execution level and coordinated across disciplines to ensure technical clarity and constructability.

Landscape Design & Documentation

This project presents a comprehensive landscape design package that reimagines the outdoor environment of a residential property as a sequence of connected yet clearly defined spaces. The master plan is structured around a central pool, creating a focal point that organizes the surrounding zones for parking, entertainment, and relaxation. Key elements include a sunken seating area, a diwaniyeh pavilion, a swing corner, a pergola deck, and a water feature, each contributing to a layered outdoor experience.

Beyond the spatial concept, the project includes a full set of technical landscape documentation. The package features a dimensioned master layout, an integrated outdoor lighting scheme, and detailed drawings for all landscape components. Architectural elements such as the entrance arch, boundary fence, wall treatments, decorative cladding, and parking pergola are developed through plans, sections, elevations, and material specifications to ensure clarity, coordination, and precise execution on site.

Open-Plan Kitchen Design

This project transforms an open residential space into a functional kitchen environment while maintaining visual connection with the living area. The apartment was delivered as a raw open shell where the kitchen zone was already defined by existing plumbing points, limiting the flexibility of relocating the layout.

Following the client's request for an L-shaped kitchen, the design organizes the workspace along two walls and introduces a marble-clad peninsula that subtly defines the boundary between the kitchen and living room while preserving openness and natural light. A full-height cabinet integrates the refrigerator and built-in appliances, while lower cabinetry beneath the window maximizes storage and maintains a clean horizontal composition.

The material palette combines muted grey cabinetry, natural wood cladding, and light marble countertops, creating a contemporary yet warm domestic atmosphere. The project is presented through floor plans, interior sections, and 3D visualizations illustrating the spatial transformation from an open shell into a cohesive kitchen environment.

Kitchen Transformation & Interior Documentation

This project reimagines an outdated kitchen as a contemporary L-shaped workspace, improving functionality while responding carefully to the existing conditions of the room. As shown in the before image, the original layout combined freestanding appliances, a central dining table, and a door that limited the efficiency of the space.

The redesign preserves the existing sink location to minimize plumbing alterations, while reorganizing the layout to form a compact and efficient work triangle between the refrigerator, sink, and cooktop. The former door opening was replaced with a window, allowing more natural light to enter the space and improving both usability and spatial clarity.

The new design introduces handleless slab cabinetry in a warm cream tone, paired with integrated appliances and light stone countertops to create a clean and contemporary atmosphere aligned with the client's vision. The proposal is documented through a dimensioned floor plan and interior sections, illustrating the spatial transformation and the detailed configuration of the cabinetry, appliances, and finishes.

Brand Identity Design — Saule Haute Couture

This project presents the brand identity design for "Saule" Haute Couture, developed to reflect elegance, femininity, and radiance. The concept draws inspiration from the meaning of the name Saule, evoking sunlight and brilliance as the core visual theme of the brand.

The logo combines a circular golden emblem with stylized sun rays and a feminine silhouette in a flowing gown, symbolizing both luxury fashion and radiant beauty. The refined gold-on-deep-blue palette reinforces the sense of exclusivity and sophistication associated with haute couture.

The identity system extends across key brand materials, including shopping bags and business cards, ensuring visual consistency and a strong, recognizable presence. The result is a cohesive brand image that communicates elegance, luxury, and timeless style.
